How it works

A Structured Process
for Lasting Impact

Every program follows a proven three-phase methodology, from design to debrief, ensuring the experience translates into real workplace change.

01
Phase 1 · Design
Needs Discovery & Tailoring

We work with your leadership team (CEO, GM, HR, department head) to define objectives, understand team dynamics, and profile participants. A tailored program is then designed and refined together to ensure full alignment before implementation.

02
Phase 2 · Facilitation
Live Observation in the Field

During activities, our facilitators actively monitor team behavior in real time, tracking communication, collaboration, leadership emergence, creativity, conflict handling, and engagement levels, while ensuring a safe, structured, and highly immersive experience.

03
Phase 3 · Debrief
Transfer Learning to Work

We unpack each activity, linking it directly to workplace realities. Through guided reflection, we help the team address observed challenges, extract key insights, and translate them into clear, actionable behaviors for improved on-the-job performance.

Common questions

Everything You
Need to Know

How large a group can you accommodate? +
Programs are designed for groups ranging from 8 to 100 participants. Each program is scaled to ensure meaningful engagement regardless of group size, from leadership teams to company-wide events.
Do participants need prior outdoor experience? +
Absolutely not. Programs are designed to be accessible to all fitness levels and backgrounds. Physical demands are calibrated to your team. Psychological and physical safety always come first.
How long does a typical program last? +
Programs range from half-day experiences to multi-day boot camps with overnight stays. For meaningful behavioral change, a full day is recommended. Leadership programs benefit from 2–3 days.
Can the program be tailored to our specific business objectives? +
Tailoring is the foundation of every program. We begin with a discovery meeting with your CEO, GM, or HR team to define needs, gather participant profiles, and design accordingly. The program is finalized collaboratively before delivery.
Where do programs take place? +
Programs can be delivered at a location of your choosing, at our private base camp in the Lebanese mountains (35 minutes from Beirut), or across outdoor destinations regionally and internationally.
What happens after the activity? Is there a debrief? +
Yes, the debrief is a core part of every program. We explain the objectives of each activity, its relevance to the workplace, how to overcome the challenges observed, and define key learnings to apply at work. This is where the real value transfers.
