Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How large a group can you accommodate? +
We design programs for groups ranging from 8 to 200+ participants. Each program is scaled and structured to ensure meaningful engagement regardless of group size.
Do participants need prior outdoor experience? +
Absolutely not. Our programs are designed to be accessible to all fitness levels and backgrounds. We calibrate the physical demands to match your team and always prioritize psychological and physical safety.
How long does a typical program last? +
Programs range from half-day experiences to multi-day expeditions. We recommend at least a full day for teams seeking meaningful behavioral change, and 2–3 days for leadership development programs.
Can programs be customized to our business objectives? +
Customization is at the core of what we do. We begin every engagement with a discovery session to understand your team dynamics, goals, and challenges — then design every element of the program around your specific needs.
Where do programs take place? +
We operate across a range of outdoor environments — forests, mountains, coastlines, and rural settings. We can work near your location or arrange travel to a destination that best serves your program objectives.
